Fields

North Soccer Club has 3 fields in North Attleborough, MA 02760:

  1. Rose Field (U14, U12) - 35 Barrows St
  2. Amvet School (U12, U10, U9) - 50 Amvet Blvd
  3. NAHS JV (U14) - 1 Wilson W. Whitty Way
