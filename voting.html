You can vote! North Soccer Club Voting rights for the election of officers to the Board of Directors is extended to all NSC members who qualify based upon a point standard as defined in the North Soccer Club ByLaws. A member of the Club shall be required to earn a minimum of 12 points during the twelve month period from July 1st through June 30th in order to attain voting privileges at our North Soccer Club Annual General Meeting the second Thursday of July.

Points shall be earned as follows:

  • Officer: 12 points
  • Head or Assistant Coach: 6 points (per session)*
  • Team Administrator: 6 points (per session)*
  • Referee Coordinator: 6 points (per session)*
  • Webmaster: 6 points
  • Field Lining: 6 points (per session)*
  • Volunteer – Club Event: 2 points (per event)
  • Attendance at Monthly Meetings: 2 points (per meeting)

(* session = Fall, Indoor or Spring)
