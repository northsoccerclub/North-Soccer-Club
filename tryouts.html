Fees for Outdoor & Indoor

As of August 2009, the fees per season for North Soccer Club are as follows:

  • Fall Season: $95 for the first player in a family; $80 for the 2nd and 3rd player (4th is free)
  • Indoor Winter Season: $200 for first player age 7-14; $155 for the 2nd and 3rd player (4th is free). $200-225 for High School players depending upon which indoor facility.
  • Spring Season: $95 for the first player in a family; $80 for the 2nd and 3rd player (4th is free)

North Soccer Club has a club specific uniform which must be purchased by each player.
