Board of Directors

The Board of Directors meet monthly on the second Thursday of the month. The Club is operated by a Board of Directors elected annually during the Summer.

The officers are as follows:

  • President: chief operating officer, presides at all meetings.
  • Vice President(s): performs duties assigned by the president or Board, presides at meetings in the absence of the president.
  • Treasurer: Chief Financial officer, maintains books and records, prepares annual tax returns.
  • Secretary: maintains Club records, serves as Registrar.
  • Director of Player Development: oversee technical aspects of games, practices, and tryouts for all players.
  • Equipment Manager: responsible for uniforms and all Club equipment.
  • Field Manager: responsible for maintenance of fields.
  • Directors For Boys/Girls: assists director of player development, liason to all boys/girls teams.

North Soccer Club Voting rights for the election of officers to the Board of Directors is extended to all NSC members who qualify based upon a point standard as defined in the North Soccer Club ByLaws.
